ITHACA APPLE HARVEST FESTIVAL

Where you can experience all things apple-related

About the Festival

The Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival is a three-day event featuring all things apple-related. The festival features apple-themed foods and drinks that you could try at stalls, live music, and other activities for families. Come join us for an experience-packed weekend!

When?

September 30-October 2

Located on the Ithaca Commons

Featured Stalls

Apple Cider Donuts

Indulge in our famous apple cider donuts! Apple cider donuts are cake-like donuts flavored with cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice, and as the name suggests, the apple cider that gave makes it sweet.

Apple Pie

Satisfy your cravings with some apple pies! Its sweet flavour and textured crust is bound to draw many in. It is generally double-crusted, with pastry both above and below the apple filling.

Hard Cider

Give our hard ciders made from locally sourced apples a shot! Hard cider is an alcoholic beverage made from fermented apples. Try it out and experience its fruitiness!
